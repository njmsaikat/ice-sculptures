Ice Gallery

A professional ice bar, ice luge, or ice sculpture makes for a premier special event.

Weddings & Love

Declare your love in ice. We can carve the initials or name of the happy couple into a piece of you choosing. Ice sculptures are an elegant addition to any wedding or anniversary celebration.

Alcohol Luges

The slide that gives your liquor a chilly ride! Luges can be themed, inscribed with a message or built to hold & chill liquor bottles. An ice luge is a great way to punch up any occasion & deliver an instantly cold shot of excitement to your event attendees.

Ice Bars

Serve chilled drinks at your next event with a custom-made ice bar. These incredible pieces can be customized to fit any occasion. Add optional liquor bottle holders or a built-in ice luge! Your guests will be awed by this one-of-a-kind display.

Corporate & School Logos

Add some flair to your next company event with ice! Display food & drink at the buffet or light up your logo. Don’t forget the college grads—they can represent their school in ice too!

Holidays

Your next holiday party will be a hit with a carving made from clear, flawless ice. For whichever holiday you observe, we can sculpt a festive image to add to your celebration.

Celebrations

Celebrate your next milestone with an ice sculpture! Carve your love for your partner in crystalline ice. Make a 21st birthday unforgettable with an ice luge. Treat your child like a princess with their own ice castle. Anything is possible!

Aquatic & Seafood Displays

Celebrate summer & the Jersey Shore with a playful aquatic ice sculpture at your next event. We can even create sea-themed displays to hold seafood, effectively keeping it fresh.

Sports

We know sports fans will stop at nothing to celebrate their beloved teams & we fully support your fandom! Order a team-themed ice sculpture or ice luge for your next tailgate or game day party.

Specialty Carvings

Celebrating a unique occasion, but still need that extra something special to set it off? We’ve got you covered. Our expert ice artist has over 30 years of experience carving custom ice sculptures, ice bars & ice luges. Contact us to discuss your perfect ice creation!
